# Tomato Basil Artichocke Soup

## Ingredients

2 cans (14.5 oz or 28 oz) of stewed tomatoes
2 cups marinated artichokes
2 T basil
1/4 cup red onion
3 cloves of garlic
2 T olive oil

## Instructions

Blend stewed tomatoes, artichokes in a food processor until smooth (depending on the size of the processor you may need to blend in seperate batches) add all to a bowl and mix together. Mince garlic, basil and onions. Add oil, garlic and onions to pan. Saut√© for one minute at a medium heat then add ingredients in the bowl and simmer for 10-15 minutes.
