# Homemade salsa

Serves 2

## Ingridients

- 2 heirloom tomato
- 2 slice of onion (red)
- 6 peperoncini
- 1/2 teaspoon or small clove of garlic
- 1/2 teaspoon of parsley or cilantro
- pinch of salt 

Chop tomatoes, mince all other fresh ingredients and mix with tomatoes then add salt.
Makes four servings.
