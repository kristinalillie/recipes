Super Foods

For more information about super foods read SuperFoods Rx by Steven Pratt, M.D. and Kathy Matthews. (Super food list and recommeded amounts from Superfoods Rx)

Eat more super foods!

Beans

Similar foods: All beans are super foods. Super easy! How much should I eat? A minimum of four 1/2 cup servings per week.

Blueberries

Similar foods: Purple grapes, cranberries, boysenberries, raspberries, strawberries, currants, blackberries, cherries, etc. Eat them fresh, frozen or dried. How much should I eat? 1 - 2 cups daily

Broccoli

Similar foods: Brussel sprouts, cabbage, kale, turnips, cauliflower, collards, bok choy, mustard greens, swiss chard. How much should I eat? 1/2 - 1 cup daily

Oats

Super similar foods: Wheat germ and ground flaxseed. Other similar foods: brown rice, barley, wheat, buckwheat, rye, millet, bulger wheat, amaranth, quinoa, triticale, kamut, yellow corn, wild rice, spelt and couscous. How much should I eat? 5 - 7 servings a day

Oranges

Similar foods: Lemons, grapefruit*, kumquats, tangerines and limes. How much should I eat? 1 serving daily *Grapefruit can cause drug interactions in some cases, so you may want to consult with your doctor before adding grapefruit to your diet.

Pumpkin

Similar foods: Carrots, butternut squash, sweet potatoes, orange bell peppers. How much should I eat? 1/2 cup most days

Wild Salmon

Similar foods: Alaskan halibut, canned albacore tuna, sardines, herring, trout, sea bass, oysters and clams. How much should I eat? two to four times a week

Soy

Similar foods: tofu, soymilk, soy nuts, edamame, tempeh, miso. How much should I eat? At least 15 grams of soy protein (30 to 50 isoflavones; not from isoflavone-fortified products) per day and divide total daily soy intake into two separate meals or snacks

Spinach

Similar foods: Kale, collards, Swiss chard, mustard greens, turnip greens, bok choy, romaine lettuce, orange bell peppers. How much should I eat? 1 cup steamed or two cups raw most days

Tea

Similar foods: None. How much should I eat? 1 or more cups daily

Tomatoes

Similar foods: Red watermelon, grapefruit, Japanese persimmons, red-fleshed papaya, strawberry guava. How much should I eat? 1 serving of processed tomatoes or similar foods per day and multiple servings per week of fresh tomatoes

Turkey (Skinless Breast)

Similar foods: Skinless chicken breast. How much should I eat? 3 to 4 servings per week of 3 to 4 ounces (max, 4 ounces per serving)

Walnuts

Similar foods: almonds, pistachios, sesame seeds, peanuts, pumpkin and sunflower seeds, macadamia nuts, pecans, hazelnuts, cashews. How much should I eat? 1 ounce, five times a week

Yogurt

Similar foods: Kefir. How much should I eat? 2 cups daily
